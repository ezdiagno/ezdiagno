About Us.

We, at EZdiagno, aim to make your medical journey as smooth as possible. Instead of constant trips to doctors clinic, send your full medical analysis directly to doctors via EZD. Digital copies ensure that your results are always secure. You can also get your results and analysis online.

Our Process.

EZdiagno is a technology based asset light company which aggregates diagnostic service providers and provides them a tech based product to offer and market their services. We intend to be technology facilitators for healthcare diagnostics units.

Our Approach.

Leveraging technology to provide best in class services is what defines our approach towards building a quality product to serve customers at large. For us customers satisfaction is paramount and through our offerings we aspire to be able to create a delighted user base.

Our Vision.

We aspire to be the best in class technology providers for Diagnostics in the country with due focus upon customer services. We want to bring a hassle free and economical diagnostics experience for our users and to serve healthcare at large.

Our Objective.

To provide a simple yet innovative interface for users across the country to discover the most reasonable pricing with best in class services for healthcare diagnostics around them. We also intend to provide data insights upon our users health records to promoting a healthy life for all.
